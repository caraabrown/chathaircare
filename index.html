Module 01

Core Routine

Built around your reality: high heat weekly, low/no heat every 3–4 days, weekly deep conditioning, finger detangling, cuticle sealing, and minimal breakage.

Cadence: High Heat = weekly · Low/No Heat = every 3–4 days

Wash Routine (7-step)

24-hr pre-poo → day-of pre-poo → cleanse → clarify (as needed) → deep condition → rinse/seal → style

24-hour pre-poo (simple + low mess)

  • Avocado oil + sesame oil (thin film)
  • JBCO/castor only on ends
  • Cap + satin bonnet overnight

Day-of hot oil (scalp focus)

  • Sesame oil base (warm-touch only)
  • Amla + brahmi/bhringraj (tiny amounts)
  • Massage 3–5 min, cap, 20–30 min under warm hood/heat cap

Non-negotiables (what drives retention)

  • Deep condition weekly (cap + heat 15–30 min)
  • Seal cuticle after DC (brief ACV rinse, then cool rinse)
  • Thin-film rule on oils/creams to avoid coating fine strands
  • Clarify only when hair feels coated / styling won’t “take”
