Module 01

Core Routine

Built around your reality: high heat weekly, low/no heat every 3–4 days, weekly deep conditioning, finger detangling, cuticle sealing, and minimal breakage.

Cadence: High Heat = weekly · Low/No Heat = every 3–4 days
Outcome target:

Softness + smoothness are the short-term signals. The long-term signal is fewer mid-shaft snaps and calmer ends (less single-strand knotting / splitting).

Module 02

GK Hair “The Best” Keratin Module

A fine-strand, high-porosity modified plan that prioritizes length retention, strength rebuilding, and straight-style longevity.

Cycle: about every 10–12 weeks (not monthly)
Non-negotiables:

Ventilation + mask + gloves. Follow your reduced heat/pass rules. Do not stack heavy oils/butters before application. Do not add DIY ingredients into GK solution.

Prep Window (48–72 hrs before)

  1. Stop oils, butters, heavy creams, and heavy silicone serums.
  2. Cleanse: clarify if coated; otherwise a solid shampoo cleanse is enough.
  3. Skip leave-in and skip oils after that wash.
  4. Hair should be clean, product-free, fully dry on application day.

Tools Checklist

  1. Blow dryer + paddle brush
  2. Fine-tooth comb for comb-through
  3. Sectioning clips
  4. Ceramic flat iron with temp control (ideal)
  5. Mask + gloves + ventilation
01

Application Day (Modified for Fine Strands)

Clean → dry → apply thinly → blow dry smooth → seal with controlled heat
One session
Plan time
Temperature + passes (your safe settings)
  • Top half (3C/finer): 350°F · 1–2 passes
  • Bottom half (4A/more resistant): 360–365°F · 2–3 passes
  • Ends: 1 pass only
  • Thin sections. No clamping hard. No repeated “chasing.”
How to avoid over-saturation
“Thin Film” Product Rule
Apply GK sparingly. Comb through. If hair looks wet/greasy or drips: you used too much. Wipe excess with gloved fingers before blow-drying.
  • 6–8 sections minimum.
  • Do not apply onto scalp.
Hold period (first 48–72 hrs)
  • No washing.
  • No oils/butters/leave-ins.
  • Avoid sweating/steam.
  • Keep hair straight; avoid tight bends/creases.

Safety rules (DIY + essentials oils)

  1. Essential oils: keep dilution low; avoid daily scalp use of peppermint/eucalyptus/lemongrass.
  2. Glycerin: use sparingly; reduce if frizz increases.
  3. Powders (chebe/ayurvedic): keep solids low on fine strands to avoid coating + friction.
  4. Keratin window: stop oils/butters 48–72 hours pre-application.
  5. Heat: ceramic flat iron preferred; follow pass limits.
